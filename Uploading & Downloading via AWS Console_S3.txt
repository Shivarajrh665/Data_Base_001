Option 1: Uploading & Downloading via AWS Console
Uploading an Object

Sign in to the AWS Console and open the Amazon S3 service.

Navigate to your desired bucket and click on its Objects tab.

Click Upload, then choose Add files (or Add folder) to select items from your computer.

Once selected, click Upload to begin.
